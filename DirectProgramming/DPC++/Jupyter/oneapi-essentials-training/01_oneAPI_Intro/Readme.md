## Purpose
The initial hands-on exercises in this notebook introduce you to DPC++ and the goal of oneAPI. Also, it familiarizes you with the use of Jupyter notebooks as a front-end for all training exercises. This workshop is designed to be used on the Devcloud and includes details on submitting batch jobs on the Devcloud environment.

## Install Directions

The Jupyter notebooks are tested and can be run on Intel Devcloud.
Below are the steps to access these Jupyter notebooks on Intel Devcloud
1. Register on [Intel Devcloud](https://intelsoftwaresites.secure.force.com/devcloud/oneapi)
2. Go to the "Terminal" in the Intel Devcloud
3. Type in the below command to download the oneAPI-essentials series notebooks into your Devcloud account
    /data/oneapi_workshop/get_jupyter_notebooks.sh
4. Navigate to oneAPI_Essentials folder and open the Welcome.ipynb, click on "Module 1 - Introduction to oneAPI and DPC++" notebook and follow the instructions
